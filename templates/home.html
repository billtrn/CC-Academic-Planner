Welcome to the Connecticut College Academic Planner!


About the CC Academic Planner and Researchers

This academic planner is part of a research project conducted by senior Computer Science major and Psychology minor Chelsea Vickers '21, advised by Professor Christine Chung, for COM 495 and COM 496. This software was designed to help students visually plan out their schedules for an upcoming semester by integrating course information from the Registrar to eliminate the potential of copying over course information over when planning a schedule.


How to Use the CC Academic Planner

  • To start building your schedule for the upcoming semester, click the "Start Scheduling!" button below.
  • On the right side of calendar page there is a form where you can indicate the subject of a course and then the dropdown below that will populate with course offerings for the department you selected for the upcoming semester.
  • Once you have selected a course, press the "Add Course" button to add the course to your schedule. After you add a course, the planner will update your calendar view so you can visualize your weekly class schedule!
  • If you would like to start over, press the "Clear Course Selection" button.
